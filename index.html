Hello!

My name is Karen-Anne Levo.
I am a graphic designer and artist based in Toronto, Ontario.

I graduated from the University of British Columbia, with a degree in Art History and a minor in Studio Arts. I then attended B.C.I.T and Emily Carr University to earn my certification in Graphic Design. In the following years I had the pleasure of working as an in-house designer and consultant for several small (but mighty!) Canadian businesses.

In recent years I've enjoyed contributing to the efforts of a marketing team for a national financial investment firm here in Toronto.

This past year I attended Bitmaker Labs, Front-End Development program where I learned the latest in responsive design and the fundamentals of Javascript.

I enjoy the challenge behind developing effective design. I value the influence that visual choices have in communicating and reinforcing messages to large, diverse audiences. Most importantly, I love the creative process. With every client, team and colleague I have worked with, the development of creative ideas always proves fun for everyone involved, which I find very rewarding.

Outside of design, I volunteer and engage in Toronto's incredible arts community. I am a practicing artist and I enjoy working in a variety of mediums that range from traditional to digital.
